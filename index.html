Welcome

Welcome to Leeroy Montessori School, where we are dedicated to enhancing overall child development and assisting children in becoming productive members of the society.

Our warm and friendly atmosphere, combined with our qualified staff and great leadership, provides a dynamic link between your child and their learning environment.

At Leeroy Montessori, we believe in raising well-rounded children by placing a thorough focus on every child’s cognitive, physical, social, and emotional development.

Why Choose Us

Why choose LMS?

Warm and Friendly Atmosphere

We create a nurturing environment where every child feels welcome, valued, and encouraged to thrive. Our inclusive culture fosters confidence and a sense of belonging.

Qualified Staff

Our team of passionate and experienced educators is dedicated to academic excellence, providing personalized guidance and support to help every student reach their full potential.

Great Leadership

Strong leadership is the foundation of our success. Our dedicated administrators ensure a clear vision, innovative strategies, and a commitment to continuous improvement for our students.

Overall Child Development

Beyond academics, we focus on the holistic growth of each child—nurturing creativity, emotional intelligence, and critical thinking to prepare them for a bright future.

Our Programs

INFANT
CENTRE

3-18 months

Every journey begins with a first step! Our Infant Centre provides a nurturing, safe space where babies grow, explore, and develop essential sensory and motor skills through engaging activities.

TODDLER COMMUNITY

18 months - 3years

Designed to foster independence and social interaction, our Toddler Community encourages hands-on learning through structured activities that enhance cognitive, motor, and problem-solving skills.

CHILDREN'S HOUSE

3 years - 5 years

A place where young minds thrive! Our Children’s House nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and foundational skills through play-based learning, preparing kids for the next stage of progressive education.

PRIMARY
SCHOOL

6 years and above

Our Primary School program blends academic excellence with character-building activities, shaping confident, knowledgeable, and well-rounded individuals ready to take on new challenges.
