Welcome

Welcome to Leeroy Montessori School, where we are dedicated to enhancing overall child development and assisting children in becoming productive members of the society.

Our warm and friendly atmosphere, combined with our qualified staff and great leadership, provides a dynamic link between your child and their learning environment.

At Leeroy Montessori, we believe in raising well-rounded children by placing a thorough focus on every child’s cognitive, physical, social, and emotional development.
